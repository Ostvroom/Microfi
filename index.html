FAQ

What is MicroFi?

MicroFi connects smaller, high-signal creators with projects. Campaigns are funded by client-locked $MICRO pools. Creators earn from those pools for approved, high-quality engagement.

What decides my reward?

Mindshare score: Social Card (followers & KOL quality) × content quality × diversity × timing, plus a tiny randomness to avoid gaming. Admins can add bonuses for exceptional posts.

What if the pool runs out?

The campaign auto-closes or the client tops up. All approved proofs remain recorded; final settlement occurs at season end.
